About Players123.com

Welcome to Players123.com – where passion meets performance in the vibrant world of racing!

Running has become a vital part of India’s wellness culture, with races of all sizes happening across the country. Since our inception in 2019, we've been dedicated to creating a trusted, innovative platform that connects and empowers the running community.

Born from the shared vision of a group of marathon enthusiasts, Players123.com has evolved through countless discussions with race organizers and runners, always embracing the latest technologies to serve our community better. Our journey began with our first race in Kochi, and since then, we've been at the heart of countless events, fostering connections and celebrating every runner's journey.

During the pandemic, we utilized this time for research and development, resulting in a refined platform that brings participants and organizers together seamlessly. At Players123.com, we bridge the gap between runners, organizers, and the larger running community, ensuring every race is a memorable experience.

Join us, lace up your shoes, and be a part of the growing movement that celebrates health, wellness, and the joy of running!
